# Dump Notes
___

### Initial Meeting

#### 1. Business Objectives
- Identify the stakeholders, and their roles
- Understand the high-level goals and objectives of the project
  - What business problems are we solving?
  - What decisions should this data enable?
  - What does a success look like? (KPIs, outcomes)
  - Who is the primary business owner and who are the consumers?
#### 2. Scope & Use Cases
- What are the key use cases for first phase?
- What is explicitly in scope and out of scope?
- Are there downstream analytics, reporting or ML use cases planned?

#### 3. Data Consumers
- Who will use the data (executives, finance, HR, analysts)?
- How will they access it (dashboards, exports, APIs)?
- What level of data granularity is required?

#### 4. Source Systems
- Whare are the primary data sources?
- Are they internal, external, or third-party? Is there any vendor delivering the data?
- How frequently does the data change?

#### 5. Data Freshness
- How current must the data be?
- Is near real-time required or batch is sufficient?
- Are there critical cutoff times? (payroll runs etc.)

#### 6. Data Quality & Trust
- What data accuracy level is required?
- Are there known data quality issues today?
- Who owns data validation and sign-off?

#### 7. Compliance & Security
- Are there regulatory requirements (GDPR, SOX, tax laws)?
- What data is sensitive or restricted?
- Who can access what level of data?

#### 8. Volume & Growth Expectations
- Current data volume and expected growth?
- Peak loads or seasonal spikes?

#### 9. Reporting & Metrics Alignment
- Are metric definitions already standarized?
- Are there conflicting definitions today?
- What reports are business critical?

#### 10. Constraints & Dependencies
- Budget or timeline constraints?
- Existing platforms or tools we must use?
- Dependencies on other teams or vendors?

#### 11. Delivery & Priorities
- What's the most valuable first deliverable?
- Is this a one-time build or a long-term platform?
- How will changes be requested and prioritized?

#### 12. Risks & Known Challenges
- What has failed in similar past projects?
- Known technical or organizational risks?
